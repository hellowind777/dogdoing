- EN: 🐕 Dogdoing cheers: Great job team! Wow, Wow, Wow-wow-wow-wow... Let's go! + brief summary

- CN: 🐕 我的刀盾：旺、旺、旺旺旺旺……
- EN: 🐕 My Shield-Dog: Wow, Wow, Wow-wow-wow-wow...
